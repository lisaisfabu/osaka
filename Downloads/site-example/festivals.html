Laughter Ritual

Unique Laughing ritual.

Crowd of people at the ritual.

The most unique and heartwarming ritual is held at Hiraoka Shrine. As the saying goes, "Fortune smiles on a merry home", so give your household the best chance of good luck in the New Year by joining the "laughing ritual" at Hiraoka-jinja Shrine. At this hilarious event, priests lead the crowd for 20 minutes of side-splitting laughter. This is based on the Japanese mythology that many gods danced and laughed to coax the sun goddess from her cave. The event kicks off with a ceremony to replace oldshimenawa spiritual barriers with new ones, and concludes with the the laughing ritual. There will also be a laughing contest, comedy performances and a lecture on mythology.

Yassai Hossai

Burning wood to honor diety.

Men running across firewood.

Festival associated with a legend of long time ago when fishermen warmed up an image of the deity Ebisu which had drifted ashore near Iwatsuta Shrine, by burning 108 bundles of firewood. Hoisting a man dressed as Ebisu on their shoulders, men run across the fire cheering "Yassai, Hossai".

Where?

Iwatsuta Shrine
Japan, 〒592-8334 Osaka Prefecture, Sakai, Nishi Ward, Hamadera Ishizuchonaka, 4-12−7

German Christmas Market

German Christmas Market at Umeda Sky Building.

The market at night.

The German-themed Christmas Market held at Umeda Sky Building is one of the largest events in winter in Osaka. Precious goods including ornaments are available in addition to mulled wine and grilled sausages. At the centre of the market, you will see a stunning illuminated tree.
